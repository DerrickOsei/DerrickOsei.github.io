ABOUT NAA SHORMEH & FRIENDS

ABOUT US

NAA SHORMEH & FRIENDS

A life impact group, Aimed at helping children, orphans, less privileged and the disabled in our society . Named after its founder CLARA NAA SHORMEH ABA DOWUONA, ‘Naa Shormeh & friends’ is a non-profit charity organization focused on improving the livelihood of orphans in Ghana. By organizing annual charity projects ‘Naa Shormeh & friends’ aims at meeting the need of orphans in its own way. To fulfill it’s mission of sending hope to the society, the organization leverage on individual as well as organizational support and believes greatly in collective effort.

 

BIRTHDAY WITH THE CHILDREN

Our maiden project, ‘BIRTHDAY WITH THE CHILDREN’, dated on our founder’s birthday is a celebration with the children of the Village of Hope Orphanage. ‘BIRTHDAY WITH THE CHILDREN’ is designed to bring games, merry making, feasting and many other fun activities to the doorstep of these children. The project also focuses on making donations (with the help donors, friends and partners) which directly meets the needs of the orphanage.

It is estimated that 140 million children worldwide are orphans. More than 34 million are from Africa and 1 million from Ghana . So Naa Shormeh & Friends as a group are here to support these children with your help.
